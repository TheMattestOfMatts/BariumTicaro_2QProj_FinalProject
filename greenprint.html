Youth Rising for Climate Action

The power to change the future is in the hands of the youth. Every action taken today builds a greener and more sustainable tomorrow.

Youth-Led Campaigns

Youth Activists Rally Worldwide for Climate Justice

Young people worldwide are leading grassroots movements, spreading awareness, and motivating communities to act on climate change.

Source: UNICEF, 2025

Fridays for Future Strikes Shake Global Leaders

The Fridays for Future movement, sparked by Greta Thunberg, mobilizes millions of students globally to demand urgent climate action from leaders and scholars, uniting voices for climate justice.

Source: Tindall, 2025

Filipino Youth Lead Earth Month Climate Protest

Youth Advocates for Climate Action Philippines organized strikes and community events during Earth Month 2025, pushing for pro-planet policies ahead of elections.

Source: Fridays For Future Philippines, 2025
